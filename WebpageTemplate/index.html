What is Locci

Locci is a location based quick note taking app.

How does it work

Locci lets you add a note based on you current location. It's super easy, super quick.


Mark your friend's place or your favorite hangout destination or just a new location you like and you might get back to.

Get the App

Where are my notes stored

Your notes are stored locally on your device. They are not sent over to any server and hence are completely in your control.
